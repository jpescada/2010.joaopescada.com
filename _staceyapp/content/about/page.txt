<h2>Hello!</h2>

My name's João Pescada. I'm an Interactive Developer in Lisbon, Portugal.

Currently I'm working at <a href="http://www.view.pt/" title="Open VIEW's website" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackPageview', '/goOut/view.pt']);">VIEW</a>, awarded Digital Agency of the Year in 2007, 2008 and 2009 by <a href="http://www.clubecriativos.com/" title="Open Clube de Criativos' website" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackPageview', '/goOut/clubecriativos.com']);">Clube de Criativos de Portugal</a>.

For the last 8 years of my life, I've been working as a designer and a developer for digital media, for clients like Algarve Convention Bureau, Casinos de Angola, Datafax, Dom Pedro Hotéis, Galp Energia, Grão Vasco vinhos, Miguel Praia, Oon Solutions, Parkalgar, RE/MAX, SATA, Star Alliance, TAP, Timex, Vale do Lobo and Zippy, among others.

Occasionally, I publish some thoughts on my <a href="http://blog.joaopescada.com/" title="Open My Blog" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackPageview', '/goOut/Blog']);">blog</a>. Feel free to leave your feedback. :)

In order to give back to the developers' community what I've been taking for a lot of years, I started sharing source code at my <a href="http://github.com/jpescada" title="Open My GitHub" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackPageview', '/goOut/GitHub']);">GitHub</a> account.

Most of the time, I'm available for freelance work. Just drop me an <a href="mailto:hi@joaopescada.com" title="Send e-mail" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackPageview', '/goOut/Email']);">e-mail</a>.
