Two different applications of Augmented Reality, both Flash based for online publishing:<br>
— TIMEX A.R. Watch: a real-looking 3D watch (with working 3D pointers) for users to experience the brand's watches on their own wrists (later used in <a href="http://www.esta-na-hora-timex.com/" title="Open Está na hora TIMEX's website" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackPageview', '/goOut/esta-na-hora-timex.com']);">Está na hora TIMEX</a>). (2009)<br>
— Airplane A.R. Viewer: a simple 3D airplane with interactive and animated parts (later to be changed for scaled down versions of real models). (2009)<br>
