Two educational games for children developed for Zippy: <i>"Apanha Apanha"</i> and <i>"Entra e Sai"</i>.<br>
In <i><a href="http://www.zippy.pt/Game/Detail/apanha-apanha_864fb38e8fce" title="Open 'Apanha Apanha' game" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackPageview', '/goOut/zippy.pt_apanhaapanha']);">"Apanha Apanha"</a></i>, Augmented Reality is created though the webcam to interact with the game.<br>
In <i><a href="http://www.zippy.pt/Game/Detail/entra-e-sai_f3b169f7640a" title="Open 'Entra e Sai' game" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackPageview', '/goOut/zippy.pt_entraesai']);">"Entra e Sai"</a></i>, the game generates a different perfect maze for each of the 5 levels, in every play.
